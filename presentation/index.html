Why do we need them?

  • Better web applications

Not convinced?

Success stories?

Bottom line:

Users are more likely to use apps that are:

  • Easily discoverable
  • Linkable (cross device and app)
  • Single click

What are the characteristics of a PWA?

  • Responsive
  • Connectivity independent
  • App-like interactions
  • Fresh
  • Safe
  • Discoverable
  • Re-engageable
  • Installable
  • Linkable

How do I build a PWA?

  • Service worker
  • Application manifest
  • Push notifications
  • CacheStorage API
  • IndexDB

Who maintains the standards?

  • World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
  • These are all new or draft standards
  • Notification, Push, Service Workers, Background Sync...
